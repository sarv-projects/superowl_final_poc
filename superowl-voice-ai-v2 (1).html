How inbound call forwarding works

Business owners forward their phone to your shared SuperOwl DID. When a customer calls, Vapi reads the SIP Diversion header (forwarding number) to identify which business it is, then starts a personalised agent using that business's config. The same system prompt from the Outbound tab is used here — with {{businessName}}, {{city}} etc. filled per-business.

🔀 Inbound Business Registry
Each business registered here gets its own AI agent on inbound forwarded calls
🔀
How this works: Each business owner configures call forwarding on their own phone number to your shared SuperOwl DID +91 80 4568 XXXX. When a customer calls the business, their phone forwards it. Vapi reads the SIP Diversion header to know which business it is, then loads that business's personalised AI agent automatically.
🔁
Outbound → Inbound loop detection is active. If SuperOwl places an outbound callback and the business owner's phone forwards it back to the SuperOwl DID (because they're busy), the system detects the loop, marks the owner as busy, and instructs the outbound AI agent to inform the customer: "The team is currently with another customer — I'll make sure someone follows up with you shortly."
