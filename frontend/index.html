How inbound call forwarding works

Business owners forward their phone to your shared SuperOwl DID. When a customer calls, Vapi reads the SIP Diversion header (forwarding number) to identify which business it is, then starts a personalised agent using that business's config. The same system prompt from the Outbound tab is used here — with {{businessName}}, {{city}} etc. filled per-business.
